# Tests

## Test 1 - App loads to onboarding [sanity]
Viewport: desktop
Description: A fresh visitor sees Grove's onboarding welcome.

Steps:
1. Open the app
2. Wait for load

Expected: A welcome dialog titled "Welcome to Grove" is visible with a name input and a "Next: plant a goal" button.

## Test 2 - Onboarding plants the first goal
Viewport: mobile
Description: Completing onboarding creates a goal and lands on the Today view.

Steps:
1. Open the app
2. Type a unique name like "Anu-<timestamp>" into the name input
3. Tap "Next: plant a goal"
4. Tap the first goal template in the list
5. Tap "Plant this goal"

Expected: The Today view greets the player by name and lists at least one tiny step with a checkmark button.

## Test 3 - Circle tab offers to start a real circle
Viewport: desktop
Description: After onboarding, the Circle tab shows the make-it-real card.

Steps:
1. Open the app and complete onboarding with a unique name
2. Click the "Circle" tab

Expected: A card titled "Make it real" is visible with "Start a circle" and "Join with a code" buttons, and five member cards are shown.

## Test 4 - Creating a circle shows an invite code
Viewport: desktop
Description: Starting a circle produces a shareable invite code.

Steps:
1. Open the app and complete onboarding with a unique name
2. Click the "Circle" tab
3. Click "Start a circle"
4. Type "The Bloomers" into the circle name input
5. Click "Create circle"
6. Wait for the circle header to appear

Expected: The Circle tab header shows "The Bloomers", an "Invite code:" chip with a six-character code, and a "Copy invite link" button. One member card is labeled REAL is not required; garden spirit tags are visible on simulated members.

## Test 5 - A step taken by one member reaches another
Viewport: desktop
Description: Two anonymous players share a circle; Actor B's step appears in Actor A's feed.

Steps:
1. Actor A opens the app, completes onboarding with a unique name, opens Circle, starts a circle named "Sync Grove", and notes the invite code
2. Actor B opens the app in a separate anonymous session, completes onboarding with a unique name
3. Actor B opens the Circle tab, clicks "Join with a code", enters Actor A's invite code, and joins
4. Actor B opens the Today view and checks off the first tiny step
5. Actor A waits up to 60 seconds on the Circle tab for the feed to refresh

Expected: Actor A's Grove Feed shows an entry saying Actor B's name took a step, and Actor B appears as a member card with a REAL ribbon.

## Test 6 - First whisperer tap asks for consent
Viewport: desktop
Description: AI features are consent-gated; the first tap opens the consent dialog, not a network call.

Steps:
1. Open the app and complete onboarding with a unique name
2. Open the Circle tab, start a circle named "Consent Grove"
3. Open the Today view, then click "Plant a new goal"
4. Click "My own goal"
5. Type "Learn watercolors" as the goal name
6. Click the "draft my tiny steps" button

Expected: A dialog titled "The Grove Whisperer" appears explaining what is sent, with a "Let it whisper" button and a "Not now" button.

## Test 7 - A resting whisperer fails warmly
Viewport: desktop
Description: When the AI steps endpoint is rate-limited, the wizard keeps the player's own steps and shows warm copy.

QA Faults:
```json
[
  {
    "method": "POST",
    "path": "/api/ai/steps",
    "status": 429,
    "body_json": {
      "error": "ai-rest"
    }
  }
]
```

Steps:
1. Open the app and complete onboarding with a unique name
2. Open the Circle tab, start a circle named "Rest Grove"
3. Open the Today view, click "Plant a new goal", click "My own goal", and type "Learn pottery" as the goal name
4. Click "draft my tiny steps"
5. Click "Let it whisper" in the consent dialog

Expected: A toast contains "The whisperer is resting" and the three placeholder tiny-step inputs are unchanged.

## Test 9 - Claiming the grove is offered after the first bloom
Viewport: desktop
Description: After her first goal blooms, a gentle account prompt appears on Today; "not now" dismisses it and it stays dismissed after reload.

Steps:
1. Open the app and complete onboarding: pick "My own goal", name it "Tiny win", remove the third step so only two steps remain, and plant it
2. On Today, complete both steps (close the bloom celebration dialog when it appears, skipping the reflection)
3. Look at the Today view
4. Click "not now" on the claim card
5. Reload the page and open Today

Expected: After the bloom, Today shows a card titled "Your grove is growing" with a "Claim my grove" button and a "not now" button; after clicking "not now" and reloading, the card does not reappear.
